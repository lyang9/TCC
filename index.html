Navajo Nation


Xin

As a graduating student I had an inner debate before going for this trip, as mid-March was supposed to be a crucial time for my graduation thesis work and job searching. "Will I fall behind the progress or lose job opportunities? Can I have peace in this week long trip?" I eventually decided that I should firdt give time to Him, because it isn't mine in the first place. I visited the Navajo Nation with a heart determining to connect with and learn form the native people as well as Pastor Lee and his wife, who was serving as missionaries. Pastor Lee's story truly inspired me to have faith in following God's leading in my life. "I am the vine; you are the branches. If a man remains in me and I in him, he will bear much fruit; apart from me you can do nothing." (John 5"15)
